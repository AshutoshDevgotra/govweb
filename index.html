Frequently Asked Questions

How does the complaint registration process work?

Simply search for the government department related to your complaint, fill out the registration form, and submit it. We will ensure that your complaint reaches the right authorities.

Yes, we take the privacy and security of your personal information seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.
